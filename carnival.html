About This Game

Carnival Hunt is a multiplayer social horror game where players take on the roles of Bunnies or the Carnival Monster in a dark, twisted carnival setting. Bunnies must work together to wind up each other's keys and escape, while the Monster hunts them down before their keys run out.

With atmospheric environments, tense gameplay, and unique mechanics, Carnival Hunt offers a fresh take on asymmetrical horror. Will you survive the night, or become part of the Carnival forever?
