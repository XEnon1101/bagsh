About This Game

Hollow Knight is a challenging, beautifully hand-drawn action adventure set in a vast, ruined kingdom of insects and heroes. Explore twisting caverns, ancient cities, and deadly wastes, battle tainted creatures, and befriend bizarre bugs, all in a haunting, atmospheric world.

Master powerful abilities, customize your playstyle, and uncover secrets as you journey through Hallownest. With tight controls, epic boss fights, and a rich story, Hollow Knight is a modern classic in the Metroidvania genre.
