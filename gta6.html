About This Game

Grand Theft Auto VI takes players to the vibrant streets of Vice City, featuring a sprawling open world, dynamic weather, and a gripping story of crime, ambition, and survival. Experience next-generation graphics, seamless character switching, and a living world filled with opportunities and danger.

Engage in heists, build criminal empires, explore new vehicles and activities, and immerse yourself in a narrative that pushes the boundaries of interactive storytelling. GTA VI sets a new standard for open-world action and freedom.
