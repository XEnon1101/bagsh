About This Game

Resident Evil Requiem is a survival horror game that plunges players into a terrifying world filled with relentless enemies, haunting environments, and gripping storylines. Step into the shoes of a new protagonist as you unravel the mysteries behind a sinister outbreak and fight for survival against unimaginable horrors.

Experience atmospheric exploration, resource management, and intense combat as you navigate through abandoned villages, eerie mansions, and underground laboratories. With stunning visuals, immersive sound design, and a chilling narrative, Resident Evil Requiem delivers a heart-pounding experience for both longtime fans and newcomers to the series.
