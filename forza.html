About This Game

Forza Horizon 5 is an open-world racing game set in the vibrant landscapes of Mexico. Experience limitless driving action with hundreds of cars, dynamic seasons, and breathtaking environments. Compete in thrilling races, explore diverse biomes, and participate in creative events with friends.

Customize your vehicles, build your own races, and discover a living world full of challenges and rewards. With stunning graphics, realistic physics, and a massive multiplayer playground, Forza Horizon 5 is the ultimate celebration of car culture.
