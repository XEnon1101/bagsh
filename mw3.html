About This Game

Call of Duty®: Modern Warfare® III continues the iconic saga with intense, cinematic single-player missions and the most expansive multiplayer experience in the franchise. Join Task Force 141 as they face off against the ultimate threat, Makarov, in a globe-spanning campaign filled with action, stealth, and explosive set pieces.

MWIII features new maps, modes, and customization options, plus the return of fan-favorite Zombies mode. Experience fast-paced combat, tactical gameplay, and a deep progression system in both solo and online play.
