About This Game

Silent Hill f is a new entry in the legendary horror franchise, set in 1960s Japan. Unravel a mysterious and terrifying story as you explore haunting environments, solve puzzles, and survive grotesque threats. Experience psychological horror and atmospheric storytelling in a world where reality and nightmare intertwine.

Written by acclaimed Japanese writer Ryukishi07, Silent Hill f delivers a chilling narrative and disturbing visuals that will keep you on edge from start to finish.
