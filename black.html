About This Game

Black Myth: Wukong is an action RPG inspired by the legendary Chinese tale Journey to the West. Step into the role of the Destined One and wield the powers of the Monkey King as you battle formidable foes, explore mystical landscapes, and uncover ancient secrets.

Featuring stunning visuals, fluid combat, and a rich narrative, Black Myth: Wukong blends myth, fantasy, and martial arts in a breathtaking adventure. Master magical abilities, transform into different creatures, and challenge epic bosses in a world teeming with wonder and danger.
