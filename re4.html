About This Game

Resident Evil 4 Remake reimagines the classic survival horror masterpiece with modern visuals, gameplay, and storytelling. Step into the shoes of Leon S. Kennedy as he embarks on a dangerous mission to rescue the president's daughter from a mysterious cult in a remote European village. Face terrifying enemies, solve puzzles, and experience heart-pounding action in this critically acclaimed remake.

With enhanced graphics, improved controls, and new content, Resident Evil 4 Remake delivers a fresh yet faithful experience for both longtime fans and newcomers.
