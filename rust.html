About This Game

Rust is a multiplayer-only survival game where players must gather resources, build shelters, craft weapons, and survive in a harsh, open world. Face off against wildlife, environmental hazards, and other players in a struggle for survival. Form alliances, raid enemy bases, and defend your own in a world where trust is rare and danger is everywhere.

Whether you choose to go solo or team up, Rust offers a unique blend of PvP, base building, and exploration. The only aim in Rust is to survive.
